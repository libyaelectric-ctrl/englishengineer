- Essential onboarding for discipline, experience, current/target English level, industry, daily goal, career goal and timezone.

- Activation: at least 70% complete onboarding and one mission.

- Crash-free sessions: at least 99%.

These are targets, not achieved results. Server-wide measurement requires a consented production analytics backend. Current metrics are local to one browser profile and contain no unnecessary personal payload.
